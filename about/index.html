About / Contact

The main purpose of this site is to post previews of upcoming anime opening and endings before they air. A lot of what I post will just be a compilation of official MVs etc, but I also post plenty of radio rips that most people wouldn’t be aware of. If you know of any previews that I haven’t posted I would appreciate it if you left a comment to let me know.

All videos posted on this site are for preview purposes only and are not hosted on this site. I will be removing links to previews shortly after they have aired.
